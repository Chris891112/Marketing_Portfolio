GENESIS G70 - Digital

GENESIS G70 Sport launching campaign has contributed to raising brand awareness within the young generations. This digital campaign was mainly focusing on delivering the look & feel of G70 to the millennials. Using multi-track techniques, contents were placed so that viewers could watch short videos and then automatically watch other videos without leaving the channel.

Veloster "Live Loud"

Veloster is the black sheep in the hyundai family of carlines. It has a one-of-a-kind asymmetrical design that centers around and expresses individuality which represents the uniqueness and challenging spirit. We focused on the influencer campaign with the keyword "individuality," which represents the common feeling of those who want to defy normality in their lives. "Live Loud" campaign focused on social channels that the younger generation like to use and this campaign shows Hyundai Motor's new attempts to millennials. (2,113,911 views)

KONA Launching

The growing number of millennials has led to increased demand for small cars. Under the concept of Urban Smart Armor, I could contribute to the launching campaign for Hyundai Motor's first compact SUV with both sophisticated and innovative style and practicality. It succeeded in increasing awareness and preference through snack content targeting the younger generation, including collaboration with Marvel.

NEXO Launching

I took part in the launching campaign for NEXO, the world's first hydrogen car. This brand film was delivered at the CES venue as a video that conveys the message that new energy will lead the future generation. Since then, 6 different USP videos including autonous parking have been produced.

belif - Design

The image above shows the display strategy that I planned to promote the image of the product as a "moisture bomb" and "honest". This strategy is to integrate the transparent water bottle and product, which were the trend in 2016, and to incorporate scientific evidence that drinking water is best for the skin. Crumps' Naturals 2.0

Planned and developped new website with high consumer accessibility, reflecting its unique brand identity. I planned a high-end website reflecting natural & warm brand image. During the wireframe stage, I focused on highlighting the natural color of the product and also delivering the comfortable website function in purchase experience. This website combines minimalist designs and user friendly functions, and our team is keep updating its personalized service functions.
